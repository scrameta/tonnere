ESP32 module (U34: ESP32‑WROVER‑E) pin usage summary and pinlist

Derived from the KiCad netlist (tonnere.net). ESP32 has a flexible GPIO matrix (most peripherals can be routed to many pins), so this is primarily a wiring pinlist: which module pads / GPIOs are used and what they connect to.

Functional grouping (wiring view)

  • Atari SIO: 8 signals
  • JTAG (GPIO matrix): 4 signals
  • SPI bus to STM32/logic: 4 signals
  • UART to STM32: 2 signals
  • Audio/control: 1 signal
  • BOOT/STRAP (GPIO0): 1 signal
  • EN (chip enable/reset): 1 signal
  • ESP.SGPI0: 1 signal
  • ESP.SGPI1: 1 signal
  • ESP.SGPI2: 1 signal
  • ESP.SGPIO: 1 signal
